About Me

I'm a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at UCLA, and I'm very fortunate to be part of Professor Harry Xu's research group. My primary research interest lies in building resilient and intelligent systems for modern applications. I focus on key challenges within large-scale distributed platforms, spanning both conventional data center architectures and the specialized infrastructure required for high-performance machine learning. My work specifically investigates how to enhance system resilience, usability, and reliability in these demanding environments.
